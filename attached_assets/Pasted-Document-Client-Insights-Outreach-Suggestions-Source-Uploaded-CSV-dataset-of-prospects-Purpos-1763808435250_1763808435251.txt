1. Ideal Client Identification Framework
Based on the dataset, the strongest prospects fall into these buckets:
A. Founders / Co-Founders
Tech founders with active product development or scaling challenges.
 They generally require:
Custom software development


Architecture improvement / re-engineering


Security hardening and compliance


Implementation support for AI features


B. CTOs / Technical Heads
They need:
Dedicated engineering support


DevSecOps, CI/CD, cloud cost optimization


Secure software design reviews (SDL)


On-demand development teams


C. CEOs of IT Services Firms
They often outsource:
Overflow development work


White-label software development


Security audits for client-facing products


Architecture review and modernization
